## What you’ll find inside

- Tuscan-style restaurants  
- Non-Tuscan restaurants (regional detours when you want something else)  
- Street food & bakeries  
- Cafés, wineries & bars  
- Markets & shops (edible souvenirs, take-home upgrades)  
- Experiences & places (classes, tastings, viewpoints)  
- Foods & dishes (so you can order with confidence)

## Freshness

Florence changes fast. This is a **2026 edition** — and you should always verify opening hours directly with venues.
